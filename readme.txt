= WHAT IS SENDBYSHARE? =

**Send By Share Solution Co. (aka SBS)** applies crowd-shipping business model by paying its SBS app users to take care of last-mile deliveries using eco-friendly vehicles. The system is capable of clustering close-by orders together into logical and time-saving routes, and matching couriers’ frequented locations with delivery routes in the neighborhood. Whether you’re a student hoping to fund your dreams, or a professional courier looking for more jobs, **SendByShare** is the right partner for you.

== Installation ==

= General instructions =

1. Upload the plugin files to the '/wp-content/plugins/sendbyshare-shipping' directory,
2. Activate the plugin through the **Plugins** screen in WordPress or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly (recommended).
3. From the Wordpress Dashboard, navigate to **WooCommerce -> Settings -> SendByShare Tab**. Click to **Connect to SendByShare** button.
4. Enter the **Secret** and **Secret Key** which provided by SendByShare then click **Save Settings**. And now, you connected your WordPress site to SendByShare system.

= Setup shipping zones =

1. Navigate to **Woocommerce -> Settings -> Shipping Tab**. Click **Add shipping zone** or edit exists zones.
2. Enter the zone name and which regions will be handled by SendByShare Service.
3. Choose **Add shipping method** and Select **Send By Share Delivery**. Then click **Add shipping method** to add SBS to this zone.
4. If you still want to setup Free shipping on SBS. Click **Edit** on **Send By Share Delivery** in Shipping methods box. You can setup the cost, enable free shipping and which minimum order amount to freeship.
